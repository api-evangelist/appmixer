Drawing on Intercom CPO Paul Adams' talk at WeAreDevelopers 2025 in Berlin, the article describes AI as the greatest technological shift since the early internet. It covers adopting rapid 3-6 month development cycles, breaking down traditional role boundaries, and competing through continuous experimentation rather than long-term planning.
